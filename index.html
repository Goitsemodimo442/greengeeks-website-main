About Green Geeks

Green Geeks is South Africa's premier garden management system, designed to help both amateur and professional gardeners create and maintain beautiful, sustainable gardens.

Our innovative digital tools leverage the latest agricultural research and AI technology to provide personalized recommendations for irrigation, planting schedules, and garden maintenance.

Founded in 2025, we're committed to promoting sustainable gardening practices across South Africa while making garden management accessible to everyone.
